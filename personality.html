Personality

Through the results of all the quizzes, I learnt more about myself than I was expecting. It displays my strengths and weakness in a range of settings, which aids with my future careers. According to the 16 Personality Test a “Protagonist” stating that although they love helping and pushing their friends, they aren’t always happy with the result (Friendships | Protagonist (ENFJ) Personality | 16Personalities, 2022). Through this, I have learnt that I must be more accepting of other’s choices even if it is something I do not want. So in terms of group settings, releasing that if others believe that my ideas are not suitable or achievable for what we are trying to accomplish, I should not take it to heart.
Also stating that these people want to help their co-workers be successful and achieve their goals (Workplace Habits | Protagonist (ENFJ) Personality | 16Personalities, 2022). This helps for when I work in team projects as I try to make sure we achieve everyone’s goals whilst achieving our main objective as a group. Similarly in the Big Five Personality Test, it states that people with a higher percentage of “Agreeableness” tend to work together with their team, rather than compete (The Big Five Personality Test, 2022).
This displays that I would work effectively in teams, and because I enjoy and stive to help others, this could suggest I could make a great leader in a team. Lastly my results on my Learning Style Test helped me become aware that I am a “Auditory” learner (What's Your Learning Style? 20 Questions, 2022). Through this I was able to realise that since I work more efficiently when music or noises are in the background, I must make it aware that I am still listening to my peers and team members.
